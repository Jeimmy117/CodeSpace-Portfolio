PixelCows

Welcome to Pixel Cows, the bovine-related community where every member has his own pixel art cow/bull avatar.

"But why cows?" you may think. Well, this project started time ago related to some game streaming. In order to achieve a better interaction between streamer and chat, a program named Stream Avatars was used to bring to life some cow and bull sprites so every person in the chat could choose their preferred one and customize it as they liked (with the provided colors and different accessories).

Our mission is to keep that bond between streamers and viewers and make it even stronger giving the viewers the opportunity to interact with the streamer and the stream itself.

At the moment this project is mostly focused on the pixel, cows as you can see above, adapting the accesories for them and creating and improving animations for each action offered by the program mentioned before. But we're wroking on it to be able to bring another animal-themed avatars to those streams, so the viewers have a wider selection to choose from.

Join us in this jorney to achieve this awesome goal and keep track of every addition to our pixlated family!
